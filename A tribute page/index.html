Wayne Rooney

Manchester United's all time leading goal scorer and a legend, his time at United coincided with some of the most succesfull period in Manchester United's history.

About The Legend



👉🏻 Wayne Rooney, in full Wayne Mark Rooney, (born October 24, 1985, Liverpool, England), who rose to international football stardom as a teenager while playing with the English Premier League powerhouse Manchester United.

👉🏻 Rooney made his professional debut with his local club Everton at age 16, becoming the youngest goal scorer in Premier League history in his first season (the record has since been surpassed).

👉🏻After two years playing for Everton, he transferred to Manchester United in 2004.

👉🏻With Manchester the precocious young striker quickly became one of the most popular football stars in the United Kingdom.

👉🏻In 2003, Wayne Rooney became the youngest player to ever represent England.He is also England’s youngest ever goalscorer.

👉🏻On October 4, 2008, in an away win over Blackburn Rovers, Rooney became the youngest player in league history to make 200 appearances.

👉🏻In 2008 & 2009, he won the England Player of the Year award.

👉🏻He is also England’s top ever goalscorer, ahead of Harry Kane(as of now).
